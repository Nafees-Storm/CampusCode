About Us

This group is for anyone in and around Luton who is interested in learning to code, improving their digital skills, or exploring opportunities in the tech industry. Whether you're just starting, changing careers, or looking to connect with others on a similar path, our free workshops and events provide a friendly, supportive environment for learning and growth. Powered by Code Features, our mission is to make coding education accessible, practical, and community-driven. Join us to kickstart your journey, meet like-minded people, and build something meaningful together.
